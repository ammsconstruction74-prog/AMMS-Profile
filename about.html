About AMMS Construction

Civil • Electrical • Plumbing Solutions

Company Overview

AMMS Construction is a professional construction company providing civil, electrical, and plumbing services for residential and commercial projects. We are committed to delivering reliable workmanship, quality materials, and efficient project execution while ensuring client satisfaction at every stage. Our approach focuses on practical engineering solutions, transparent communication, and dependable site management.

Management Profile

Owner

Muhammad Misbah Ur Rehman is the founder and owner of AMMS Construction. He oversees project planning, site execution, client coordination, and quality control across all civil, electrical, and plumbing works. With hands-on involvement in projects, he ensures that each job is completed with strong workmanship, proper materials, and timely delivery. His leadership focuses on reliability, transparent dealings, and long-term client satisfaction.
